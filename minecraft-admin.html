Minecraft Server Explaination

We are here to explain to you on how to make a Minecraft server in a couple of quick and easy steps

Getting started with Minecraft at Flextech High School.

  1. First thing on how to start your server is to go and get canarymod(you can get it at canarymod.net) Download the link called "CanaryMod(Server)".
  2. After you have completed that and it has downloaded, find CanaryMod in "Downloads" folder and drag it into your "Documents" folder.
  3. Once you move CanaryMod into your "documents" folder, double click it. If you are not able to launch it, you will get this dialog:

    Press the question mark (?) in the bottom left corner.
