Minecraft Client Tutorial

How to Obtain and Setup the Client

  • First make sure that minecraft.net and mojang.net are both unblocked(contact Mr. Gillis if needed)
  • Then go to Minecraft.net
  • Assuming you do not have a Minecraft account, you will need to click on register in the right hand corner to set up a Minecraft/Mojang account
  • You must first enter your e-mail address, then make a password, and lastly enter your date of birth
  • Then you will need to buy the game
  • After that is done all you need to do is press the download button to download the launcher
  • Then you must start the launcher and enter your username and password
  • Next you will have to chose your Minecraft version(if you are trying to use the Canarymod server use 1.8.0) and launch the game causing it to download
  • Finaly launch the game and enjoy!
